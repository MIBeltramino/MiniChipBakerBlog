A Little About Me

My name is Tanya, I'm currently a stay at home wife and student. I live with my wonderful husband in Argentina and am studying online through eCampus New Zealand.

I'm an expat who knows wherever you go in the world, food brings people together. Everytime someone gets you to try their new recipe or one that's been passed down generations it's a small act of trust. Making a new recipe is much like getting a new haircut, you know if your partner actually likes it. Everytime I make a new favourite recipe that's a good day even if the kitchen looks like a small hurricane went through it.
