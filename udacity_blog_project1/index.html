Welcome to Our Kitchen

Simple, easy, nourishing recipes that require only a handful of ingredients. Baking is science, but it's also messy, fun, and creative. The beautiful thing about baking is that it's usually made to be shared. Baking can be an excuse for a catch up with the friend or a gathering point for celebration.

Most of these recipes allow you to have a little flexibility that allows you to add your own spin with chocolate chips, dried fruits, or nuts if you like a little texture.

For those of you with a keen eye, you might notice some of the recipes don't actually require you to turn on the oven, which makes them ideal for the hotter months.
