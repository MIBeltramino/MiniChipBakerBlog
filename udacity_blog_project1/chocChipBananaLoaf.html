Ingredients

  • 2 overipe bananas
  • 2 Tbs baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 Cups flour (optional: sub for gluten-free flour)
  • 1/2 Cup organic sugar (optional: sub for coconut sugar or 1/4 cup white sugar and 1/4 cup brown sugar)
  • 250ml milk of your choice
  • 150g butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 Cup chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Melt the butter in a small pan on minimum to medium until it starts to turn a light brown, taking care not to let it burn.
  2. Mash bananas.
  3. Add milk, butter, and sugar then whisk until combined.
  4. When the mixture has cooled whisk in both eggs.
  5. Sift in the baking powder, baking soda, and flour.
  6. Mix with a silicone spatula until combined.
  7. Gently fold in chocolate chips
  8. Bake on fan for 30mins at 180C.

Tips

  • It can be tempting, but don't add the egg until the butter is cool enough or you might just be adding scrambled egg to your loaf.
  • Mix gently, mixing too much will result in a rubbery texture.
