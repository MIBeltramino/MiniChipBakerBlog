Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice/corn based gluten free flour
  • 1 Tbs baking powder
  • 1 overipe banana (or 1 1/2 small ones)
  • 150ml rice milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 Tbs oil (if not using non-stick)

Directions

  1. Mash banana.
  2. Whisk in egg and milk.
  3. Sift in baking powder and flour.
  4. Mix gently.
  5. Heat the oil in the pan.
  6. Turn the heat to medium-low.
  7. Drop half a cup of pancake batter into the pan and cook for two mintues either side.
  8. Serve and top with honey and yogurt for extra decadence.

Tips

  • Warning! The banana makes them brown very quickly. Don't leave these cooking unattended! These are a little more delicate than the standard pancakes due to the banana and gluten-free flour.
  • The first one will indicate if they need a little extra! Sometimes the pancakes might need a little more flour or milk if they're a little runny or stiff.
