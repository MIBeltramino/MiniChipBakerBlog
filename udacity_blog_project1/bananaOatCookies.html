Ingredients

  • 2 overipe bananas
  • 1 2/3 Cup oats
  • 1/3 Cup dried cranberries (optional, but highly recommended)
  • Other dried fruits, chocolate chips or nuts (optional)

Directions

  1. Mash bananas.
  2. Mix in oats, cranberries or other mix-ins.
  3. Scoop dollops of the mixture on a tray and flatten with a fork. (Larger cookies will result in a chewier texture).
  4. Bake for 18mins at 180C.

Tips

  • If you prefer crunchier cookies instead of chewy cookies you can leave them in a couple of minutes longer.
  • If your bananas are ripe but not quite overipe, I recommend baking them in the oven until the skin is blackened before mashing them.
